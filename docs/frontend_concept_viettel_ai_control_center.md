# Frontend Concept

## Concept duoc chon

**Viettel AI Control Center**

Day la concept chinh thuc de thong nhat cho toan bo viec nang cap frontend ve sau.

Muc tieu cua concept nay la bien he thong hien tai thanh mot giao dien:

- Dep va an tuong khi demo cho lanh dao
- Mang ban sac Viettel ro rang nhung tinh te
- Hien dai, cao cap, co chat AI va security
- Van giu tinh enterprise, nghiem tuc, de van hanh

Concept nay se la chuan tham chieu cho cac quyet dinh thiet ke tiep theo nhu:

- Theme sang toi
- Mau sac he thong
- Icon
- Motion
- Sidebar
- Login page
- Dashboard
- Bang bieu va form nghiep vu

## Ly do chon concept nay

Trong cac huong thiet ke co the di, `Viettel AI Control Center` la huong can bang tot nhat giua:

- Tinh tham my
- Tinh thuong hieu
- Tinh ung dung thuc te
- Muc do an toan khi trinh bay cho moi truong tap doan

Concept nay khong qua don gian den muc nhat nhòa, nhung cung khong qua futuristic den muc loe loet hoac kho van hanh. No tao duoc cam giac day la mot san pham cong nghe nghiem tuc, duoc dau tu, co nang luc van hanh thuc te.

## Tinh cach thuong hieu

Concept nay phai truyen tai duoc cac tinh chat sau:

- Executive
- High-tech
- Secure
- Modern
- Reliable
- Disciplined

Neu mo ta bang mot cau ngan:

> Mot trung tam dieu hanh AI mang phong cach Viettel, hien dai, chuan xac, an toan va du sang de thuyet phuc lanh dao.

## Cam giac tong the can dat duoc

Nguoi dung khi nhin vao he thong can co cam nhan:

- Day la mot nen tang AI monitoring thuc thu
- Giao dien nay thuoc mot tap doan lon, khong phai mot mau dashboard co san
- He thong trong rat co dau tu va co dinh huong san pham ro rang
- San pham nay nghiem tuc, bao mat, va dang tin cay

## Dinh huong hinh anh

Frontend se duoc dinh huong theo ngon ngu hinh anh cua mot **control center**:

- Nen sach, gon, hien dai
- Cac khoi thong tin duoc to chuc ro rang
- Diem nhan thi giac tap trung vao du lieu, camera, canh bao va realtime
- Co cac chi tiet goi nho den AI vision, signal, network, scan, tracking

Khong di theo huong:

- Qua giong landing page marketing
- Qua vui tuoi hoac consumer
- Qua nhieu mau
- Hieu ung loe loet

## Dinh huong mau sac

### Chien luoc mau

He thong se su dung:

- **Do Viettel** lam mau nhan thuong hieu
- **Trang, xam sang cao cap** cho light theme
- **Xanh den, xanh than sau** cho dark theme
- Mau trung tinh de giu giao dien sang va nghiem tuc

### Nguyen tac su dung mau

- Mau do khong trai deu toan bo giao dien
- Mau do chi dung cho CTA, active state, diem nhan va nhan dien thuong hieu
- Khu vuc du lieu nen uu tien trung tinh de de doc va cao cap hon
- Mau danger cho loi nghiem trong can tach voi mau brand khi can

### An tuong mong muon

- Light theme: sach, sang, cao cap, chuyen nghiep
- Dark theme: sau, manh, hien dai, control room, monitoring center

## Dinh huong theme sang toi

Theme sang toi la mot phan cot loi cua concept.

### Light theme

- Danh cho thao tac nghiep vu hang ngay
- Uu tien su ro rang, de doc, sach se
- Co chat enterprise cao cap

### Dark theme

- Danh cho monitoring, camera, dashboard, realtime
- Tao wow factor khi demo
- Van phai giu duoc tinh nghiem tuc va de nhin lau

### Nguyen tac

- Khong dung den tuyet doi
- Khong dung dark mode kieu neon gaming
- Uu tien xanh den sau, surface tach lop ro, text sang diu
- Accent do Viettel chi dung de nhan co chu dich

## Dinh huong typography

Typography can giup giao dien trong "ra san pham" hon:

- Tieu de chac, gon, ro
- KPI va so lieu de quet nhanh
- Meta text, subtitle, status text sach va co hierarchy
- Cam giac ky luat, chinh xac, khong mem yeu

Typography khong nen qua trang tri, ma can phuc vu muc tieu:

- De doc
- Sang
- Chuyen nghiep
- Hien dai

## Dinh huong icon

He thong se co 2 lop icon:

### Lop 1: Icon he thong chung

- Dung icon line don gian cho cac thao tac thong thuong
- Vi du: them, sua, xoa, tim kiem, loc, cai dat

### Lop 2: Signature icons

Tao icon rieng cho cac khu vuc cot loi:

- Face recognition
- Camera AI
- Attendance
- Spoof alert
- Realtime monitoring
- Department network
- Admin control
- Event review

### Phong cach icon

- Net manh
- Hoi vuong
- Mang ngon ngu ky thuat
- Co motif scan frame, signal, grid, node, tracking box
- Toi gian nhung co dau an rieng

Muc tieu la tao su khac biet ma van giu duoc kha nang doc tot o kich thuoc nho.

## Dinh huong motion

Motion trong concept nay la:

**Subtle premium motion**

Nghia la:

- Co chuyen dong de tao suc song
- Co wow factor nhe
- Nhung luon tiet che, muot va co chu dich

### Hieu ung nen co

- Fade va slide nhe cho card, dialog, panel
- Hover transition muot cho button, menu, navigation item
- Alert pulse nhe cho canh bao realtime
- Scan line hoac gradient shift rat cham o cac khu vuc nhan
- Background motion rat nhe o login va dashboard

### Hieu ung khong nen co

- Animation nhanh
- Qua nhieu doi tuong cung chuyen dong
- Particle day dac
- Hieu ung neon gay
- Motion lam giam kha nang doc du lieu

## Dinh huong nen dong va hoa tiet

Concept nay cho phep dung nen dong, nhung phai cuc ky kiem soat.

### Hoa tiet phu hop

- Luoi cong nghe mo
- Cac duong tin hieu rat nhe
- Cac node ket noi tinh te
- Scan line cham
- Goc bracket goi nho den tracking frame
- Pattern line hoac radar nhe

### Khu vuc nen ap dung

- Login page
- Dashboard top section
- Camera panel
- Notification va alert zones

## Dinh huong bo cuc

### Login

Login se duoc thiet ke nhu mot:

**Secure Access Portal**

Can dat duoc:

- An tuong ngay tu lan dau mo he thong
- Nhin la thay chat AI, security, monitoring
- Sang, gon, cao cap

### App shell

App shell se la:

**Executive navigation frame**

Dac diem:

- Sidebar dep, co active state co ban sac
- Top account area gon, ro, chuyen nghiep
- Theme switcher va language switcher tich hop dep
- Notification area sang va co tinh he thong

### Dashboard

Dashboard se la:

**AI Monitoring Command View**

Dac diem:

- KPI ro trong tam
- Camera va realtime la khu vuc trung tam
- Alerts va events co muc uu tien thi giac cao
- Bieu do va so lieu duoc sap xep nhu trung tam dieu hanh

### Data pages

Data pages se theo huong:

**Enterprise data workspace**

Dac diem:

- Bang bieu sach, thoang, ro
- Filter va thao tac gon, chinh xac
- Dialog, dropdown, date picker dong nhat
- Giam cam giac kho cung cua form nghiep vu

## Muc do sang trong duoc chon

Neu chia cap do giao dien thanh 3 muc:

1. Enterprise sach, an toan
2. Enterprise cao cap, co ban sac
3. Futuristic manh, co tinh pho dien

Concept nay chon muc:

**2. Enterprise cao cap, co ban sac**

Day la muc phu hop nhat voi boi canh tap doan, de thuyet phuc lanh dao, va van an toan trong trien khai thuc te.

## Nguyen tac thiet ke xuyen suot

Moi quyet dinh frontend ve sau nen duoc doi chieu voi cac nguyen tac sau:

- Co to ra chat Viettel khong?
- Co tao cam giac AI control center khong?
- Co du sang va chuyen nghiep de demo cho lanh dao khong?
- Co qua pho truong hoac qua generic khong?
- Co de doc, de dung, de van hanh khong?
- Co nhat quan voi light va dark theme khong?

Neu mot thay doi dep nhung lam mat tinh nghiem tuc, hoac dep nhung qua giong template thi khong phu hop concept nay.
